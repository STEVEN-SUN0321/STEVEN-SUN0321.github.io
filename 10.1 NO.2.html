A Taiwanese singer, actor, songwriter and producer. Since his debut in 2000 with his first album "Jay", his music has been popular in Greater China and Chinese communities around the world, and has had a significant impact on the Chinese music scene. He is one of the most influential and famous Chinese singers in history, and is known as the "Asian Pop King".

Jay Chou has won almost all the important music awards in the Chinese music scene, including 15 Taiwan Golden Melody Awards and 2 MTV Asia Awards. In 2003, he appeared on the cover of the Asian version of "Time" magazine and then launched six world tours. In 2009, he was selected as one of the 25 most influential people in Asia by CNN. The album "The Greatest Work" released in 2022 won the championship of the world's best-selling album certified by the International Federation of the Phonographic Industry IFPI. In addition to music, he also started his film career in the movie "Initial D" and has since been involved in many other film projects. Jay Chou also manages his exclusive record and agency JVR Music.
