Jay Chou has won many important awards at the Taiwan Golden Melody Awards and is one of the most important figures in the Chinese music industry. He has won the Golden Melody Award for Best Mandarin Male Singer three times (2005, 2007, and 2008).

In addition, he has also won awards such as Best Album, Best Composer, and Best Producer. In 2004, Jay Chou won the Golden Melody Award for "Album of the Year" for the first time with his album "Ye Huimei", which marked his comprehensive rise in the Chinese music industry
